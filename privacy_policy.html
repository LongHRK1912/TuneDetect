Privacy Policy – TuneDetect

Last Updated: July 24, 2025

TuneDetect is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our app uses data and what information, if any, is collected when you use our service.

1. Microphone Access

TuneDetect uses your device’s microphone solely to capture ambient audio for the purpose of detecting and identifying music playing around you. This audio is processed temporarily and is not stored on the device or any server.

2. Third-Party Services

We use the ACRCloud SDK to recognize and identify music. Audio data is transmitted to ACRCloud only for the purpose of music recognition and is not stored, shared, or used for any other purpose.

3. Advertising

TuneDetect does not display advertisements within the app.

4. Data Collection and Deletion

The app does not collect or store any personal data. Therefore, no data deletion is required or applicable.

5. Age Restrictions

This app is intended for users aged 13 and above.
